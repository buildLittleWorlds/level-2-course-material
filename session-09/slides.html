The Redesign Steps

1. Title: "Sentiment Analysis" → "Restaurant Review Analyzer"
2. Description: technical jargon → plain English for a restaurant owner
3. Examples: random sentences → real restaurant reviews
4. Output: "POSITIVE / NEGATIVE" → "Happy Customer / Unhappy Customer" + advice
5. Visual: bare decimal → confidence bar

The Output Transformation

    if label == "POSITIVE":
        mood = "Happy Customer"
        if score > 0.95:
            advice = ("This customer loved the experience! "
                      "Consider thanking them publicly.")
        else:
            advice = ("This customer had a good experience. "
                      "A simple thank-you goes a long way.")

    confidence_bar = "█" * int(score * 20) + "░" * (20 - int(score * 20))
    return (
        f"Overall Impression:  {mood}\n"
        f"Confidence: {confidence_bar} {score:.0%}\n"
        f"\nSuggested Action:\n{advice}"
    )

Name the Concept

PROMPT ENGINEERING
AND HUMAN-AI INTERACTION
Prompt Engineering
Designing inputs and framing to get useful outputs from a model.
We changed examples, descriptions, and output labels.
Human-AI Interaction
Designing how humans and AI systems communicate.
We made output actionable, added confidence bars, wrote for a specific audience.

Vocabulary

Prompt engineering — designing the entire experience around an AI model, not just the chat prompt
UX (user experience) — how a person experiences and interacts with a tool
Audience — the specific person you're designing for
Human-AI interaction — the design of how people and AI systems work together

Real AI products spend more time on UX than on the model.
This is why AI companies hire designers, not just engineers.
