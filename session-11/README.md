## Narrative Role

The refinement. Students take their Session 10 builds and make them better through peer feedback and systematic testing. Back in Session 3, they broke models to find their limits. Tonight they break each other's Spaces — to make them better. Same method, different purpose. The concept: **the experimentation loop** — the difference between a prototype and a product.

## What This Session Covers

Students improve their Session 10 projects through peer feedback and systematic testing. They swap projects, try to break each other's Spaces, and use what they learn to iterate toward a polished final version. The Big Question: how do you make something good into something great?

## Connections

- **Builds on Session 10: Build Your Own from Scratch** — Students arrive with working (or partially working) Spaces. Tonight is about making them better through the same kind of testing they've done all semester.
- **Calls back to Session 3: What Models Can't Do** — The adversarial testing mindset from Act I returns, but now directed at improving each other's work rather than exposing model limits.
- **Bridges to Session 12: Demo Day** — Students prepare to present their research journey. The iteration they do tonight is the last step before presenting.
