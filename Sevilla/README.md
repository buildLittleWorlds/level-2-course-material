## Where You're Headed

By the end of this course you need three things:

1. **Three Spaces** that explore a focused research question about animation and AI — the first one simple, the second more ambitious, the third your best work. You have two animation Spaces already. See SPACE-PROMPTS.md for building guides.
2. **A research journal** that tracks your observations, experiments, and thinking week by week alongside the building. This is the part that hasn't kept up.
3. **A research brief** — a short written paper (2–4 pages) that states your research question, connects it to real published work, describes what you built and tested, and draws careful conclusions. See [Prea's research brief](../example-student-prea/research-brief.md) for the model.
