## What This Session Covers

Students compare three sentiment models side by side — one trained on movie reviews, one on Twitter, and one on product reviews. They find inputs where the models disagree and figure out why different training data leads to different predictions. The concept: Model Evaluation. The Big Question: if three models give three answers, which one is right?
