## What This Session Covers

Students take the Sentiment Showdown models from Session 4 and test them on six different text domains — medical, legal, social media, poetry, product reviews, and news. They discover that models trained on one kind of text can fail badly on another, learning firsthand about overfitting and domain shift. No new Space this session; students reuse the Session 4 Showdown Space.
