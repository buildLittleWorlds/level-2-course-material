## What This Session Covers

Students present their projects to the group, explaining what they built, what they learned, and what they would do differently. They compile their work into a portfolio — Hugging Face Spaces, Colab notebooks, and GitHub repos — that shows what they can do. The concept: Reflection and Portfolio. The Big Question: what did you build that you are proud of?
